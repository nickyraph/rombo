Short History

Rombo Nation (formally Rombo Crew) was founded in 2019, by ten students from Bsc.BICT programme in Moshi Cooperative University with the purpose of developing ICT Projects and cooperating in social life, having fun during the process.

Why "Rombo"?

In the earliest periods of our formulation, we used to meet at our President's residency, a place quite far from the university, hence the name "Rombo", symbolizing the distance from Moshi Town to Rombo district.
